Who Are We?

North Texas Palapas is based in Gainesville, TX, with over 15 years of experience building palapas in the Dallas/Ft. Worth metroplex. We build indoors, outdoors, commercial and residential properties. Other areas of specialty include: pergolas, tiki bars, tiki huts, outdoor kitchens and bars, custom rustic furniture, cedar wood work, bamboo and more! We are also licensed to work electronically and everything is professionally installed.

At North Texas Palapas we use the finest materials - all natural red cedar wood and authentic palm branches. In addition to natural thatch, we also offer synthetic palms, which are more durable over the outdoor elements and can last 20+ years. The sturdy cedar frame in palapas is guaranteed to last 10 years, and natural palm leaves are guaranteed to last 7-8 years before needing to be replaced.

Contact us for more information regarding estimates or any questions you may have!
