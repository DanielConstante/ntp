Who Are We?

North Texas Palapas is based in Gainesville, TX, with over 15 years of experience building palapas in the Dallas/Ft. Worth metroplex. We build indoors, outdoors, commercial and residential properties.

Other areas of specialty include: pergolas, tiki bars, tiki huts, outdoor kitchens and bars, custom rustic furniture, cedar wood work, bamboo and more! We are also licensed to work electronically and everything is professionally installed.
