Our Services

Palapas

At least two cedar posts (depending on size), sturdy wooden frame and either natural palm leaves or synthetic palms for the thatch. We can construct them to the size you have in mind! Popular sizes are 10'x10', 12'x15', and 20'x20' and can be round, square or rectangular.

Read More
Tiki Bars

Resistant cedar wood and natural bamboo create a tropical atmosphere perfect for relaxing and unwinding. Typical size is 8' long x 3' wide and can include thatch above, if desired.

Read More
Umbrellas

Includes natural or synthetic palm leaves, wooden frame, single cedar post, 3' round cedar table, foot rests, and up to 4 in-ground stools around the umbrella.

Who Are We?

North Texas Palapas is based in Gainesville, TX, with over 10 years of experience building palapas in the Dallas/Ft. Worth metroplex. We build indoors, outdoors, commercial and residential properties.

Other areas of specialty include: pergolas, tiki bars, tiki huts, outdoor kitchens and bars, custom rustic furniture, cedar wood work, bamboo and more! We are also licensed to work electronically and everything is professionally installed.

From Our Blog

It all started when...

The skill set of making palapas was passed down in my family through the generations with my grandparents and uncles living in cabanas in Tamaulipas, Mexico. We lived in various palapas as houses, and my mother even delivered me underneath one! Years later, it was a natural and easy decision to make my family's heritage into a business.

How many years of experience do you have?

Where do our materials come from?

It is a very interesting question! Our materials come together from Texas and Mexico. We import natural palm leaves from Tamaulipas, and everything arrives here 2-3 weeks from the time of ordering. Once all the materials are prepared, then North Texas Palapas can start construction!
